Personally, Its My Story

Being born and raised in the island of Ceylon, I truly appreciate the brief memories of my past. I was too young to hold a camera at the time when my family would hop from one place to another in the midst of social disorder. Soon, I had to leave my country to find a safe home elsewhere. Those days were challenging; to put it mildly, makes me to be more grateful to life in the beautiful city of Toronto.

During my college years, I wrote journals. Of course, no one read; but myself. The writing started to pile up and repetitive to my taste. Then I began narrating poetry. My friends and I enjoyed them. Despite everything, my language skills were inadequate at the time to produce anything significant. At some point, I found the camera to be a perfect compliment.
